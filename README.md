# 🌌 Edumia – A New Dimension for Minecraft (NeoForge 1.21.1)

**Edumia** is a large-scale fantasy dimension mod that adds an entirely new world separate from vanilla Minecraft.  
Explore unique biomes, interact with diverse races, unlock racial attributes, discover custom creatures, gather new materials, and shape your character into a true adventurer of Edumia.

> **Status:** Early Alpha — Active Development  
> **Requires:** NeoForge 1.21.1  
> **License:** MIT (or your choice)

---

## ✨ Features (Current)

### 🌀 **New Dimension: Edumia**
A fully separate world with its own:
- Biomes  
- Trees & foliage  
- Structures (future expansion planned)  
- Atmosphere & ecosystem  

### 👤 **Playable Races**
Join a race and your character permanently takes on:
- Size changes  
- Racial attributes  
- Unique appearance variants  
(Current races include Humans, Elves, Demons, Fairies, Gensai, Ogres, Orcs, and more.)

### 🌿 **Custom Blocks & Items**
Hundreds of new decorative and functional blocks:
- Wood variants  
- Stone & brick styles  
- Plants & worldgen blocks  
- Early-stage race materials  

### 🧍‍♂️ **NPCs & Creatures**
Custom mobs with unique behaviors:
- Butterflies, dragonflies, and ambient creatures  
- Racial NPCs  
- Staged future support for:
  - Mounts per race  
  - Dungeon monsters  
  - Bosses  

### 🌍 **Biomes**
Every biome in Edumia is hand-crafted:
- Tropical regions  
- Volcanic deserts  
- Mystical forests  
- Frozen tundras  
- Jungles & caves  
Planned expansions include sky biomes and sub-biomes.

## 🔧 Installation

1. Install **NeoForge 1.21.1**  
2. Download the latest release from the **Releases** page  
3. Place `edumia.jar` into your `mods` folder  
4. Launch Minecraft with NeoForge
